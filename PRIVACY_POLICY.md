# Privacy Policy for The Kiddo Log

**Last updated: 2025-12-17**

The Kiddo Log ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our digital platform, The Kiddo Log (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our services for parents, teachers, and schools, we may collect the following types of information:

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Names, email addresses, and contact details for parents, teachers, and school administrators.
- **Child Information:** Names, attendance records, learning milestones, and daily activity logs shared by authorized users.

### B. User-Generated Content
- **Media:** Photos and videos uploaded to document daily activities or events.
- **Communications:** Messages and updates shared between schools and parents.

### C. Technical Data
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App, such as feature usage and access logs.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- **Service Delivery:** To facilitate communication and share updates about children's activities, attendance, and milestones.
- **Notifications:** To send real-time alerts and updates regarding school events or daily reports.
- **Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's functionality and user experience.
- **Security:** To authenticate users and prevent unauthorized access.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services to support our App's infrastructure. These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies. Specifically, we use:

- **Google Firebase:** We use Firebase services (including Firestore, Cloud Storage, Authentication, and Remote Config) for backend infrastructure, user authentication, data storage, and feature management.
    - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The Kiddo Log is a tool designed for use by adults (parents, teachers, and administrators) to track information about children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
